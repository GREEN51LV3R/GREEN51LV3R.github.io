INTRO | JOURNEY

Hey everyone! I'm Aakash Kokate, a passionate cybersecurity researcher, bug bounty hunter, and pentester. As an IT security specialist, I strive to align security architecture with operational goals, ensuring the highest level of protection. I'm constantly learning about the latest threats and technologies, staying one step ahead.

In my free time, I enjoy playing chess and writing low-content books that explore the delicate balance between social and personal life. Here are some of the skills I've developed: Network Security, Web Application Security, Identity and Access Management, Cloud Fundamentals, Digital Forensics, and more.

I'm currently expanding my knowledge of SIEM using Splunk and scripting with Bash and Python. Beyond technical expertise, I possess strong project management, communication, and critical thinking skills. I'm also fluent in English, Hindi, and Marathi.

When I'm not immersed in cybersecurity projects, you can find me playing CTF games on platforms like HacktheBox, TryHackMe, and OverTheWire. Don't forget to check out my cybersecurity newsletter "Security With Me" and my blog on Medium for valuable insights on staying secure online.

Thanks for stopping by! Feel free to reach out if you'd like to connect or discuss anything related to cybersecurity.

"The funny thing about wishes. Sometimes all it took to make them come true was a first step."

Like many others, I had dreams of achieving greatness in a specific field. And so, with just a spark of curiosity, I began my quest to become a cybersecurity genius.

It all started with that magical moment of holding a laptop in my hands for the very first time. I knew close to nothing about it, except how to power it on and off. But as the months passed, a whole new universe revealed itself—I discovered the joy of playing games on this mystical device. And guess what? The internet, a realm of endless possibilities, awaited me, promising to fulfill my gaming desires.

Immersed in the gaming world, I stumbled upon cheat sheets—oh, the intrigue! How did they work? Why did they work? These questions marked my first steps into the world of cybersecurity, or maybe they were just happy accidents. Who knows?

Driven by mischief and curiosity, I dived into countless blogs and videos, each click fueling my passion. Tinkering and experimenting, I sought out loopholes in games, which led me to the captivating realm of hacking. Equipped with newfound knowledge, I installed hacking tools, even though I had no clue how to use them. Yet, a twist of fate came when I discovered a blog about wireless hacking. Like a phoenix, the spark within me reignited. I embarked on a quest, devouring books, enrolling in courses, and seeking advice from experienced mentors.

Though faced with unresponsive experts and unhelpful resources, I refused to surrender. Dedication fueled my extensive research as I sought the right path and honed my skills. I joined a prestigious cybersecurity institute in Pune, immersing myself in certified ethical hacking and becoming a Computer Hacking Forensic Investigator. Meanwhile, I completed my bachelor's degree in computer science.

To further sharpen my abilities, I engaged with platforms designed for cybersecurity enthusiasts, such as TryHackMe and HackTheBox. I reveled in captivating capture the flag competitions, honing my expertise. Now, the time has come to venture into the world, transforming my dream into reality.

But that's not all—I yearned to give back to the community, raise awareness about cybersecurity, and document my extraordinary journey. Blogging and writing newsletters became my creative outlets. Unexpectedly, writing embraced me, leading me to pen a book and embrace the title of author. Although unpublished, I diligently work to bring my book to life. Driven by my zealous quest for awareness, I launched a podcast and YouTube channel.

During this exciting phase, I received an offer to work as a trainee cybersecurity researcher—a golden opportunity that honed my research skills and broadened my perspectives. This is only the beginning, my friend. There are countless surprises, twists, and turns awaiting me. The divine or whims of fate will shape my path. Rest assured, I'm committed to continuous improvement and contributing to the field of cybersecurity.

Who knows? You might even become a part of my story or perhaps the charming sidekick instead of the villain. Don't worry, though—I'm pretty certain you won't be the latter. If you crave more details, funny anecdotes, or simply want to enjoy a delightful cup of tea or coffee, let's connect.
